Our Mission

Building connection through shared experience

The Chosen Connection was created to ensure no child feels alone in their journey through grief. We believe that when kids who share similar experiences come together, something powerful happens — they realize they're not the only ones, and they find the courage to keep going.

Through outdoor adventures, community events, and volunteer-driven programming, we give families a place to belong and children a reason to smile.
